How It Works
From signed contract to power on in 6–8 weeks.
Most of that time is permits, engineering, and your utility — not us. Here's the breakdown.
01

Free Assessment

We analyze your bills, roof, shading, and rate structure. If solar doesn't make sense, we say so. Same week.

02

Engineering & Permits

Electrical engineering and structural engineering drawings are prepared for your specific roof. City permit submitted. This step takes 3–5 weeks — it's the city and engineers, not us.

03

Professional Install

Once permits are approved, our licensed electricians mount, wire, and inspect. Most installs: 1–2 days on your roof.

04

Utility Interconnection

We submit your interconnection application to your utility provider. Once approved, your meter is set to bi-directional and your system goes live. 1–2 weeks.
