Good to Know

Do you need a panel upgrade?

Not every home needs one. We determine what's required during the free assessment:

✅ Panel Has Enough Capacity

200A panel with available breaker space = standard dedicated circuit, no upgrades needed. Most common for newer homes.

⚡ Need Load Management

Panel near capacity? The Wallbox Power Boost (+$699) monitors real-time usage and dynamically adjusts charging speed. No breaker trips, no panel upgrade.

🔌 Need a Subpanel

Main panel full but sufficient amperage? We add a subpanel in your garage — new circuit space for charger and future needs. Quoted during assessment.

🔄 Need Full Panel Upgrade

Older homes (100A/125A) may need a 200A upgrade — future-proofs for EV, battery storage, hot tubs, and more. Quoted during assessment.
