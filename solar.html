Net Metering
The grid becomes your free battery.
Your meter literally runs backward. Summer surplus pays for winter usage.
1

Summer: Deposit

Panels produce more than you use. Surplus flows to the grid as credits. Your meter runs backward.

2

Credits Build

Every extra kWh = 1 credit. Credits roll over month to month. No expiry within the year.

3

Winter: Withdraw

Shorter days = less production. Draw from banked credits. Pay only the net difference — often $0.
