How We're Different

Four things most installers won't tell you.

We use our own electricians.

Every Stellar install is performed by our in-house team of certified electricians and roofers, supervised by a Red Seal master electrician. We don't subcontract. That means the person on your roof is someone we trained, someone we trust, and someone we're accountable for.

We'll tell you not to go solar.

About 10% of the homes we assess don't make financial sense for solar — too much shade, wrong roof angle, or electricity usage too low to justify the investment. We tell those homeowners the truth and walk away. We'd rather lose the sale than sell you something that doesn't pay for itself.

We show you the real math.

Our proposals include 30-year financial projections with adjustable rate assumptions. You see exactly what you'll save, when you break even, and what happens if electricity rates go up or down. No inflated promises. No made-up savings numbers.

We don't disappear after install.

Your system comes with monitoring. If your panels underperform, we know before you do. Warranty claims go through us, not a call center. We've been in Edmonton since 2018 and we plan to be here for the next 30 years — the same lifespan as your panels.
